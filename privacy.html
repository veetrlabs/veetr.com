4. Tracking and cookies

We use a self-hosted Mautic instance for tracking and marketing automation. The data is stored on DigitalOcean servers located in Amsterdam, Netherlands. Analytics tracking is only enabled after you accept cookies in our cookie banner.

We also load Google Fonts to display the site’s typography. This may involve requests to Google’s servers (fonts.googleapis.com / fonts.gstatic.com), which can process your IP address and device information. If you prefer to avoid this, you can block third-party requests in your browser or ask us to provide a self-hosted font option.

Cookie categories: essential cookies required for site functionality, and optional analytics/marketing cookies used by Mautic. Optional cookies are disabled until you consent.

5. Payment processing

Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your payment card details. Stripe acts as an independent controller for payment data under its own privacy policy.

Order data collected via Stripe may be sent to our self-hosted Mautic instance via Stripe webhooks for transactional email automation and order communication.

6. Data retention

We keep personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described above and to comply with legal obligations under EU and Czech law. Typical retention periods are:

- Accounting and tax records (including invoices and order records): 10 years.

- Customer support communications: 3 years after the last interaction.

- Marketing contacts: until consent is withdrawn or 2 years of inactivity, whichever comes first.

- Analytics data: 14 months.
